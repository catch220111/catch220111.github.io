STEM Instructor | Viterbi K-12 Center

Project Overview

Location: Los Angeles, CA

Timeline: [October 2025 - December 2025 (10 weeks contract)]

Objective: To deliver high-quality STEM education through specialized curricula in programming and applied sciences for K-12 students.

The Challenge

The primary challenge involves "translating" complex technical principles—ranging from computer logic to aerodynamic lift—into engaging, age-appropriate modules that maintain scientific rigor while fostering genuine curiosity.

Curriculum & Instruction

I currently lead two distinct courses designed to build fundamental technical literacy:

  • Python Programming: Instructing students on the fundamentals of coding, including data structures, logic gates, and functional programming applications.
  • Applied Sciences: Developing and teaching modules on diverse topics including:
    • Sustainable Energy: Exploring solar, wind, and organic energy systems.
    • Aeronautics & Drones: Explaining the physics of flight and remote-piloted vehicle mechanics.
    • Human Biology: Investigating biological systems and their relationship to environmental stressors.

Values & Learning

This role has refined my Technical Communication skills. As an engineer, the ability to explain how a system works to a non-technical audience is vital for securing stakeholder buy-in and managing multidisciplinary projects.

Key Takeaway: Education is the first step in engineering—to solve global sustainability or space exploration issues, we must first build the intellectual infrastructure to support those solutions.
