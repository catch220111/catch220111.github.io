Refining Purposes : Finding the purpose of the mission.

It's always amusing to be away from the city to enjoy one's leisure by onself. Places I've grown up are relatively developed region which I loved living in, yet with excess amount of lights, it's often hard to find a night sky full with stars. While I was on my doordash runs, I happened to be at a small town called Aurora, Oregon–which was one of the order's destination. I often look up the sky whenever the weather is clear, but I haven't been able to see Big Dipper ever since 8th grade when I was volunteering at Kokuryo Observatory in South Korea. Stars truly amaze me and have always been the reason defining my goal of my life. With a log of distractions in my life, this visual stimuli helped me realize that I haven't been true to myself and given enough time to think about who I am and what I want in a while. In this page, I would like to write about myself at least untill of January 23rd 2026–especially on who I am and what I desire.
